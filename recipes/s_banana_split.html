Banana Split Shake

Home

Description

One of our favorite foodie trends is taking a beloved recipe or dish and giving it a new twist. This recipe takes all the flavours of a classic banana split (strawberry, pineapple, banana, coconut and chocolate) blended into a luscious, retro milkshake.

Ingredients (1x)

  • 4 large scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup frozen sliced strawberries
  • ½ cup crushed pineapple with juice
  • ¼ cup chocolate syrup, or as needed
  • Gay Lea Real Coconut Whipped Cream
  • 4 Maraschino cherries

Steps

  1. Combine ice cream, banana, strawberries, pineapple and chocolate syrup in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Divide shake between 2 large (or 4 smaller) glasses.
  3. Squirt a generous amount of coconut whipped cream on top.
  4. Garnish with additional chocolate syrup and a cherry (if using).
  5. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
