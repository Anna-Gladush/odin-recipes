Creamy Banana Milkshake

Home

Description

This banana milkshake recipe is a delectable treat! It's creamy, rich, and sweet. I recommend this for a morning drink.

Ingredients (1x)

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon caramel topping

Steps

  1. Combine milk, ice cream, banana, and cinnamon in a blender; blend on low speed until the mixture reaches the consistency of a thick milkshake.
  2. Pour milkshake into a cup and top with whipped cream and drizzled caramel.
  3. Enjoy!
